About AgriLink

AgriLink started as a simple idea: make it easier for farmers to reach buyers, and for buyers to find fresh, trustworthy agricultural products. Today it connects farmers, retailers, vendors, and logistics partners on one platform.

Our Mission

We aim to remove the barriers that keep farmers from getting fair prices, and buyers from getting quality produce. By using a simple online marketplace, we make agricultural trade faster, more transparent, and more accessible for everyone.

How AgriLink Works

  • Farmers and vendors register and list their products
  • Buyers browse listings and market prices
  • Both sides connect directly through the platform
  • Logistics partners help move products to market

Our Story

AgriLink began as a school project focused on solving a real problem faced by local farming communities: limited access to reliable buyers and markets. It has since grown into a working prototype of what a full agricultural marketplace could look like.
